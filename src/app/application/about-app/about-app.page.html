E-Collect is a mobile application designed for the collectors of Sorosoro Ibaba Development Cooperative (SIDC) to efficiently process payments from cooperative members with outstanding loan balances. Users of this application are required to adhere to the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), ensuring the responsible handling of personal information. All data within the application will be safeguarded to maintain confidentiality and security. © 2025. Sorosoro Ibaba Development Cooperative. All rights reserved.
